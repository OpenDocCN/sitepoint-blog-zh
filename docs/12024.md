# 痴迷于 Google PageRank

> 原文:[https://www.sitepoint.com/google-pagerank-obsessions/](https://www.sitepoint.com/google-pagerank-obsessions/)

每次我写一篇搜索引擎优化的文章，我都会收到一堆来自网络营销人员的评论，他们显然痴迷于谷歌页面排名，以至于他们真的相信页面排名会影响一切，从排名到谷歌的搜索引擎优化，再到一个网站的整体在线影响力。

显然，不管像迈克尔·加里、亚伦·沃尔、丹妮·苏利文和其他知名 SEO 建议多少次不要纠结于页面排名，总会有人骄傲地展示他们成功获得更高的页面排名，或者当他们的网站页面排名下降时感到害怕。上周，我遇到了一位评论者，他非常确信 scraper 网站帮助他的网站获得了更高的 PR，以至于他几乎变成了 scraper 的拥护者。

很多站长不知道的是，其实有两个完全不同的东西叫做“PageRank”。每个人都痴迷的是工具栏 PageRank，这真的无关紧要，因为:

*   **What you see is not what you get** . PageRanks are computed continuously so when you see a PageRank 5 that site could in fact have a PageRank 7 Or 2 ( [Matt Cutts](http://www.dullest.com/blog/more-info-on-pagerank/) ).

    > Continuous calculation of page rank Some machines accept the input of Google's PageRank algorithm and calculate the final PageRank. Therefore, at any given time, a url in Google system has the latest PageRank, which is the result of running the calculation with the input of the algorithm. Sometimes, the internal PageRank values are exported so that Google toolbar users can see them.

*   **Toolbar PageRank updates do not influence serps** ( [Matt Cutts](http://sphinn.com/story/74952#c54521) )

    > are just toolbar PageRank updates. Even if you don't show much PageRank, Google still has 200+ other signals that we use in ranking. It is definitely common for websites with lower page rankings to rank higher than those with higher page rankings-this often confuses those who pay too much attention to page rankings, and they don't pay attention to other factors that search engines may use to rank pages.

*   **Google Lowers Manually PageRanks for Sites That Are Selling Links** ( [Danny Sullivan](http://searchengineland.com/official-selling-paid-links-can-hurt-your-pagerank-or-rankings-on-google-12360) )

    > So I searched Google, and they confirmed that the PageRank scores of some websites selling links are decreasing. In addition, Google said that some websites that sell links may eventually be deleted from its search engine, or punished, causing its ranking to drop.

*   **it does n't determine your Google footprint.** [Criticizing Steve Rubel's Take on PageRank](http://www.ninebyblue.com/blog/is-pagerank-the-ultimate-measure-of-online-influence/) Importance, Vannessa Fox Writes:

    > If there is one thing that PageRank does not have, it is the determination of your Google footprint. The internal "real" PageRank is not even like this. There are many factors that determine your Google footprint. His discussion in the comments further deepened the misunderstanding of PageRank. He agrees that someone in the comment said that "PageRank is the sum of all other metrics" is not. This is one measurement plus a bunch of other measurements.

[![Don't judge a site by its pagerank](../Images/9f10a6f4d0276d79743cd5322081359d.png "Don't judge a site by its pagerank")T2】](https://www.sitepoint.com/wp-content/uploads/2009/05/pagerank.png)

唯一重要的是谷歌分配给一个网站的内部 PageRank，但是除了谷歌没有人知道。

长话短说:拥有高排名没什么神奇的，如果你是通过黑帽或灰帽技术(比如使用 scraper 网站或其他坏邻居来提高排名)来获得高排名，那就更没什么神奇的了。无论如何，你永远不会真正知道一个网站真正的页面排名是多少，即使你使用[这样的工具](http://www.digpagerank.com/  )检查 700 个数据中心的谷歌页面排名来“测试”它。

当然，你可以优化你的网站以获得更高的页面排名、更多的流量和更好的搜索结果，但是，正如 Vanessa Fox 所说:

> 如果你“优化”了你的网站，使你的相关关键词排名第一，但你的网站对搜索者来说没有吸引力，这个排名将完全没有意义，因为那些新的访问者会点击搜索结果，而不是参与你的网站。
