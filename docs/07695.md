# 了解如何在 Photoshop 中创建牛仔布和拉链

> 原文:[https://www . site point . com/learn-how-to-create-denim-and-zippers-in-Photoshop/](https://www.sitepoint.com/learn-how-to-create-denim-and-zippers-in-photoshop/)

Photoshop 提供了无限的设计可能性。你可以把任何东西变成一件漂亮的艺术品，尤其是在排版方面。有这么多不同的排版风格和效果，您可以在您的项目中使用。使用专业排版不仅是一种展示你创造力的方式，也是一种磨练你的 Photoshop 速度和能力的方式。

所以，今天我将向你展示如何在 Photoshop 中创建牛仔布和拉链文字效果。我们将学习如何使用和操作一些有趣的技术来达到预期的效果。我们将使用各种纹理和混合选项。开始设计吧！

**资源:**

[织物纹理](http://fudgegraphics.deviantart.com/art/Fabric-Textures-Pack-95442514)

[牛仔裤纹理](http://naldzgraphics.net/freebies/10-free-high-quality-jeans-textures/)

**最终结果:**

[![](../Images/7dc4e55f5da9de0356b8a861e1e0360c.png)T2】](https://www.sitepoint.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/Final.jpg)

**第一步**

打开 Photoshop，新建一个宽 575 像素、高 415 像素的文件。

[![](../Images/6159031149085d5977c25aa2ebed6403.png)T2】](https://www.sitepoint.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/Step-11.jpg)

**第二步:**

选择油漆桶工具，用颜色#2a4176 填充背景。

[![](../Images/294c7023b21a363d6f680b0c1a4c29c2.png)T2】](https://www.sitepoint.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/Step-22.jpg)

**第三步:**

打开深色牛仔裤纹理，粘贴到你的文件。现在，点击“图像”>“调整”>“色调/饱和度”，使它有点灰色。

[![](../Images/b89818affc361a8b9fc71d78a39477d8.png)T2】](https://www.sitepoint.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/Step-3.jpg)

**第四步:**

选择钢笔工具，画一个大致的形状，如下图。

[![](../Images/1465e0e96b9d1e375205c9e2851dfcd5.png)T2】](https://www.sitepoint.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/Step-41.jpg)

**第五步:**

现在，右击路径并选择“进行选择”然后，简单地复制并粘贴选定的区域。

[![](../Images/d2a8a6bc1a9dd22dbd363f2b113cbd4b.png)T2】](https://www.sitepoint.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/Step-51.jpg)

**第六步:**

现在我们将添加一些阴影和斜面和浮雕到我们的深色牛仔裤层。点击“图层”>“图层样式”，使用下面的设置添加一个阴影。

[![](../Images/489d6a4cf6c14fc8d0ff35106362a86d.png)T2】](https://www.sitepoint.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/Step-6a1.jpg)

现在，使用下面的设置添加一个斜面和浮雕层。

[![](../Images/6b64fbdcf40c5cee3cfc4544729a3b60.png)T2】](https://www.sitepoint.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/Step-6b1.jpg)

**第七步:**

现在，打开蓝色牛仔裤纹理，使用钢笔工具选择它，就像我们之前做的一样。

[![](../Images/e6e0251fe53002d22ef05e2302acfacd.png)T2】](https://www.sitepoint.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/Step-71.jpg)

**第八步:**

点击“图层”>“图层样式”>“斜面和浮雕”

[![](../Images/08bb4e4f3f236f5468cadd71221e0c2a.png)T2】](https://www.sitepoint.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/Step-8a.jpg)

使用颜色#131530 添加一个“内部阴影”。使用以下设置。

[![](../Images/c8f71c8b8c3f73ed52805e89148d57fb.png)T2】](https://www.sitepoint.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/Step-8b1.jpg)

添加一个颜色#000000 的“内部发光”。

[![](../Images/f3673d1bf06f7aa9613a140cc7215437.png)T2】](https://www.sitepoint.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/Step-8c.jpg)

最后，用#ffffff 颜色添加一些外发光。

[![](../Images/cd1b2a5f2562bfce022c12494bf0af7c.png)T2】](https://www.sitepoint.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/Step-8d.jpg)

**第九步:**

现在，我们将添加缝线。所以打开你的笔刷面板，按照下面的指导调整笔刷大小和间距。

[![](../Images/ecc0c0f1b95e92fa7ac67d64faadda4e.png)T2】](https://www.sitepoint.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/Step-9a.jpg)

现在，点击“形状动态”，调整设置，并使用这个刷子在我们的蓝色牛仔裤区域。

[![](../Images/4503dbf8e2d06660e62e27d6f5e20aac.png)T2】](https://www.sitepoint.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/Step-9b.jpg)

**第十步:**

现在，添加一些阴影，内阴影，外发光，斜面和浮雕。以下是所有四个选项的设置。

[![](../Images/4f3f822ea7ad9e910fb3ed2b37fe72a7.png)T2】](https://www.sitepoint.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/Step-10a.jpg)

[![](../Images/4bade228e58a606384f92c0469ec34e8.png)T2】](https://www.sitepoint.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/Step-10b.jpg)

[![](../Images/6a9495f3f1047810e393762c76260913.png)T2】](https://www.sitepoint.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/Step-10c.jpg)

[![](../Images/c8606a85a0aedbb917e61ea4367b49f4.png)T2】](https://www.sitepoint.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/Step-10d.jpg)

**第十一步:**

选择文本工具，使用#ff6c13 颜色在中心添加主要文本。

[![](../Images/6dd709196ee68e5a0fd437296c8da313.png)T2】](https://www.sitepoint.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/Step-111.jpg)

**第十二步:**

在我们开始设计我们的文本之前，我们需要用牛仔布纹理创建一些图案。为此，只需打开牛仔裤图片，点击“编辑”>“定义图案”

[![](../Images/58011d51a460105019d48cd8a12713ae.png)T2】](https://www.sitepoint.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/Step-121.jpg)

**第十三步:**

现在点击“图层”>“图层样式”>“斜面和浮雕”

[![](../Images/357b34feb11537fe746bb4cc7b57c7f6.png)T2】](https://www.sitepoint.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/Step-13a.jpg)

[![](../Images/92c2e242a30df426a58d79a490ba1369.png)T2】](https://www.sitepoint.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/Step-13b.jpg)

然后，使用以下设置添加一个内阴影。

[![](../Images/5b85b1f2f502c559c1fee4762bcb64f1.png)T2】](https://www.sitepoint.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/Step-13c.jpg)

添加内部发光。

[![](../Images/5a01df854e19068f623d61c9f8f143a7.png)T2】](https://www.sitepoint.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/Step-13d.jpg)

点击“图案叠加”并添加一个图案。

[![](../Images/c7667aebf257e000a402796e09443582.png)T2】](https://www.sitepoint.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/Step-13e.jpg)

然后，添加如下设置的阴影。

[![](../Images/6875abc2ae1f60c30a044f5f8aeff780.png)T2】](https://www.sitepoint.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/Step-13f.jpg)

**第十四步:**

现在，让我们开始为我们的文本效果制作拉链。选择矩形工具，创建一个小矩形，填充#383838，改变混合模式为“叠加”

[![](../Images/af43bedd77b99bee7274894b62cad93e.png)T2】](https://www.sitepoint.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/Step-141.jpg)

**第十五步:**

创建一个小矩形，点击“图层”>“图层样式”>“渐变叠加”

[![](../Images/023b12bbe9284fa6ed94f6ee3e1818eb.png)T2】](https://www.sitepoint.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/Step-15a.jpg)

现在选择钢笔工具创建一个粗略的形状，并填充颜色#ffffff。

[![](../Images/0b18c29d838fabec444d39a96bbd7b3c.png)T2】](https://www.sitepoint.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/Step-15b.jpg)

使用下列值添加笔触和渐变。

[![](../Images/0a9c44d08a1bb856301520930faf13b2.png)T2】](https://www.sitepoint.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/Step-15c.jpg)

[![](../Images/c80aa3aa27149c069fe3e58cd4a68315.png)T2】](https://www.sitepoint.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/Step-15d.jpg)

现在，只需根据需要多次复制上面的图层，就可以得到拉链效果。左侧拉链齿的冲程角度保持为 180 °,右侧拉链齿的冲程角度保持为 0°。

[![](../Images/5dfc9a649f4715ed647fe4c9389972f6.png)T2】](https://www.sitepoint.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/Step-15e.jpg)

**第 16 步**

让我们从拉链的把手或挂钩开始。选择钢笔工具，创建以下基本形状，并填充颜色#ffffff。

[![](../Images/58ac2fab4170d3c2f28e53f86cd9a53e.png)T2】](https://www.sitepoint.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/Step-16a.jpg)

现在点击“图层”>“图层样式”>“斜面和浮雕”

[![](../Images/a16fcc1be42b2721beab8c109566ca0e.png)T2】](https://www.sitepoint.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/Step-16b.jpg)

使用以下设置添加内部阴影。

[![](../Images/b636c867195ef98d17d92f5357423c56.png)T2】](https://www.sitepoint.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/Step-16c.jpg)

然后，添加渐变叠加。

[![](../Images/61b06e5ceb906842ac33a38eed0863a2.png)T2】](https://www.sitepoint.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/Step-16d.jpg)

选择圆角矩形工具，在中间创建一个小矩形，填充颜色#ffffff。然后，添加斜面和浮雕，内阴影，渐变叠加，和阴影。下面是所有四种图层样式的设置。

[![](../Images/53aac782c5566f78b1d2a41884da07ab.png)T2】](https://www.sitepoint.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/Step-16e.jpg)

[![](../Images/c0a0cb2ab9dd8cdce2bd516418180dd7.png)T2】](https://www.sitepoint.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/Step-16f.jpg)

[![](../Images/b9f99d00d7491bf31b43a1d56b4fe483.png)T2】](https://www.sitepoint.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/Step-16g.jpg)

[![](../Images/d69462c73dd9a0a1953d9121c0624e1e.png)T2】](https://www.sitepoint.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/Step-16h.jpg)

现在，用钢笔工具创建另一个形状，填充#e8e8e8。然后，使用下面的值添加斜面和浮雕和阴影。

[![](../Images/9d6f76f32c9702e7190e6fd8a2601622.png)T2】](https://www.sitepoint.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/Step-16i.jpg)

[![](../Images/149fe339f020b53a471b7ca9c3e32322.png)T2】](https://www.sitepoint.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/Step-16j.jpg)

现在，只需调整把手的尺寸，将其放在拉链末端的合适位置。

[![](../Images/338703e32a1737c0d4b95407965fd83c.png)T2】](https://www.sitepoint.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/Final1.jpg)

就是这样。我们的牛仔和拉链文字效果都做好了。我希望你们喜欢它。
